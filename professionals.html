Coming Spring 2026

For Legal & Family Professionals

We're building Clearly with documentation and professional review in mind.

Clearly is a co-parenting app launching in Spring 2026. From the start, we're designing the platform to produce clear, neutral, time-stamped records that support legal, mediation, and professional review processes.

We believe co-parenting tools should generate documentation that's useful — not just for parents, but for the professionals who sometimes need to review it.

What we're building

Clearly will include:

  • Shared custody calendars with complete scheduling history
  • Expense tracking with timestamps and receipt attachments
  • Communication tools designed for clarity and documentation
  • Export functionality for PDF and CSV records

Designed with documentation in mind

Every feature in Clearly is being built with the understanding that records may need to be reviewed by attorneys, mediators, custody evaluators, or courts. That means:

  • All records are time-stamped and preserved
  • Exports include complete context, not excerpts
  • System actions are clearly distinguished from user actions
  • The platform does not interpret or analyze behavior

For attorneys, mediators, and evaluators

We're building Clearly to support your work:

  • Clear records that reduce ambiguity
  • Consistent export formats for easier review
  • Full communication context, not cherry-picked messages
  • Documentation designed for professional standards
