For Professionals

A calmer coordination system for families — plus a parenting plan builder clients can complete before they meet with you.

Clearly is designed to reduce conflict, not inflame it. Less back-and-forth. Clearer decisions. Safe to recommend when clients need structure — not another place to argue.

Who uses Clearly

Separated and divorcing parents who need a structured way to coordinate. Professionals — attorneys, mediators, parenting coordinators, therapists — use our tools to help clients get organized before and during the process.

How professionals use it

1 Client completes the Parenting Plan Builder independently. Free, no account. Covers custody schedules, holidays, expenses.
2 They get a structured summary. A shareable document they can bring to you or share with their co-parent.
3 Less back-and-forth in your meetings. Start with a baseline. Identify gaps. Focus on decisions that matter.
4 Ongoing coordination (optional). Clients can use Clearly for continued coordination after agreements finalize.

What clients get

  • A structured parenting plan summary they can complete on their own
  • Clarity on custody schedules, holidays, and expenses before meetings
  • Fewer messages and less back-and-forth with their co-parent
  • A document format that supports professional review

Common questions

Does Clearly provide legal advice?

No. Clearly is a coordination tool, not a legal service. It helps families organize information and communicate more effectively, but all legal decisions should be made with the guidance of qualified professionals.

Can Clearly records be used in court?

Clearly produces time-stamped, unaltered records of communication and agreements. Whether any document is admissible depends on jurisdiction, case type, and judicial discretion. We provide factual records; evidentiary determinations remain with the court and counsel.

Is there a cost for the Parenting Plan Builder?

The Parenting Plan Builder is free for anyone to use. The full Clearly app is currently in private beta with limited access.

How does the app handle data privacy?

We take privacy seriously. User data is stored securely and is not shared with third parties. Each parent has their own account with access only to shared family information. For details, see our Privacy Policy.

Can I recommend Clearly to clients?

Yes. Many professionals direct clients to the free Parenting Plan Builder as a starting point for organizing their parenting plan. For the full app, clients can request private beta access.
