It's a lot to sort out

Custody schedules sound simple until you actually try to make one. Then it's:

  • What does "every other weekend" actually mean?
  • Who gets Thanksgiving this year?
  • What time do handoffs happen?
  • What if the schedule doesn't fit our work lives?
  • How do we handle summer break?

And that's before you even get to expenses, medical decisions, and everything else.

No wonder it feels overwhelming.

Most templates don't actually help

You can find custody schedule templates online. But they're either too generic to be useful, or so complicated they make things worse.

What you actually need is a way to think through the decisions — your schedule, your holidays, your situation — and end up with something clear you can both reference.

That's what the Parenting Plan Builder does.

A free tool to help you get it sorted

The Parenting Plan Builder walks you through the key decisions:

  • Custody schedule — weekly patterns that actually work
  • Holidays — who gets what, and when it alternates
  • Handoffs — times, locations, expectations
  • Expenses — how to split costs for the kids

At the end, you get a clear summary you can share with your co-parent, attorney, or mediator.

No account required. No email required. Just answers.
