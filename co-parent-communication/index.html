Why conversations keep going sideways

When communication broke down in your relationship, it didn't magically fix itself when you separated. The same patterns followed you out the door.

Maybe you explain yourself before they've even finished. Maybe you avoid conflict until it explodes. Maybe you're keeping score of every imbalance. Maybe you just want to fix it and move on — but they won't let you.

These patterns aren't flaws. They're habits. And once you see them, you can start to change the dynamic — even if the other person doesn't.
