Communication

From issue to resolution — without the back-and-forth.

Co-parent conversations often spiral. That's why Clearly uses a structured approach inspired by the Harvard Negotiation Framework to move "Topics" through to resolution.

You focus on the issue. AI helps keep the tone neutral. And conversations actually reach a conclusion.

  • Structured topics that guide you toward resolution
  • AI rewrites your message in a neutral tone
  • Your original words stay private — only the summary is shared
Schedule

Your custody calendar, simplified.

See your custody schedule, holidays, events, and swaps all in one place. Both parents always see the same calendar — no more confusion.

  • Pre-loaded custody patterns (50/50, 2-2-3, and more)
  • Holiday custody with odd/even year swaps
  • Sync to Apple Calendar or Google Calendar
Expenses

Splits calculated automatically.

Set your expense split ratio once — based on your agreement — and Clearly does the math for every expense. No spreadsheets required.

  • Flexible split ratios (50/50, 60/40, etc.)
  • Attach receipts for documentation
  • Track who owes what at a glance
Easy Setup

Get started in minutes.

Choose from common custody patterns or create your own. Set up holidays, invite your co-parent, and you're ready to go.

  • Pre-loaded schedules for quick setup
  • Share a family code to connect
  • Changes sync instantly for both parents

And so much more

Emotion stays out of it

Write what you feel. Clearly's AI rewrites it in a neutral tone — so your co-parent hears the message, not the frustration. Your original words stay private.

Holidays handled automatically

Set up holiday custody once — including odd/even year swaps — and Clearly handles it forever. No more last-minute texts about who has the kids for Thanksgiving.

Sync with any calendar

Subscribe to your custody calendar in Apple Calendar, Google Calendar, or Outlook. Share the link with grandparents, babysitters — anyone who needs to know.

Track every shared expense

Log expenses, attach receipts, and see who owes what at a glance. Splits are calculated automatically based on your agreement.
