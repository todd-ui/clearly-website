How it works

Built to change conversations, not just host them.

Four principles that make co-parenting communication actually work.

Slows you down

AI rewrites your message in neutral language before you send. Most escalation happens in the first draft.

Narrows the scope

Topics, not threads. One issue stays open until resolved. No message avalanches or subject changes.

Removes the subtext

Strips out phrases that trigger defensiveness—the "as usual," the "once again." Just the facts.

Enforces structure

You define rules in advance—response windows, topic categories. The system enforces them consistently.

Built on principles from the Harvard Negotiation Project—the same framework used in international diplomacy and high-conflict mediation. Adapted for the most difficult negotiation of all: co-parenting with someone you'd rather not talk to.
