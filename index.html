The Clearly Method

You both love your kids. That's not the problem.

The problem is that every conversation becomes positions vs positions. What you're asking for vs what they're asking for. It sounds like conflict — but underneath, you often want the same thing.

The Clearly Method draws from interest-based communication — how real conflict actually gets resolved. It's woven into the experience, subtle and natural. You just write. Clearly does the rest.
